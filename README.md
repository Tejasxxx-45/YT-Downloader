# YT Downloader

A **fast, multi-threaded YouTube video and playlist downloader** written in Python.  
Supports **custom video quality selection**, **playlists**, **threaded downloads**, and **custom storage paths**. Perfect for terminal enthusiasts who want a powerful downloader without a GUI.

---

## Features

- ✅ Download **single or multiple YouTube videos**  
- ✅ Download **entire playlists**  
- ✅ **Select video quality**: 144p, 240p, 360p, 480p, 720p, 1080p, or best available  
- ✅ **Threaded downloads** to speed up multiple videos  
- ✅ **Custom download folder**  
- ✅ **Automatic error logging** (`download_errors.log`)  
- ✅ **Sanitized filenames** to avoid issues with illegal characters  
- ✅ **Lightweight terminal interface**, no GUI needed
